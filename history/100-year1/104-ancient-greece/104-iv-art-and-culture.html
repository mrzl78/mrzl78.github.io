“Priestess of Delphi”, John Collier, 1891

Famous oracle predictions from Delphi:
  • Oedipus: you will kill your father and marry your mother. Not knowing he was adopted he left his beloved foster parents, only to bump into his real father (whom he killed) and marry a queen who happened to be his mother.
  • Croesus: “if you cross the river, a great empire will be destroyed.” (more or less) → he thought of the Persian one, but it was his own.
