Minoans, what we know
  • on Crete
  • Europe’s first cities
  • palace culture (Knossos)
  • Seafaring nation, traders, rich and peaceful → no fortifications around palaces (Crete isolated), no warrior class; probably high status of women
  • script (undeciphered: from the Phaistos Disk, Linear A. readable: Linear B)

Myth and archaeology

Heinrich Schliemann, who discovered Mycenae in the 19th century, found what he wanted to find: “proof” that the Homeric epics (the Illiad and the Odyssey) were based on historic facts. So, when he found the gold mask shown above, he felt the strong need to connect it to the mythological king Agamemnon, without even knowing when it was made, let alone who it really depicted.
